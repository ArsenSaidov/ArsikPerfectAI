This GitHub organization is dedicated to safe, ethical, and respectful
collaboration surrounding the conceptual and educational framework of
the Arsik Perfect AI and The Arsik Continuum.

## Our Commitment
- We create a welcoming and supportive environment.
- We respect people of all backgrounds and ages.
- We focus on learning, creativity, and constructive dialogue.

## Expected Behavior
- Be kind and respectful.
- Offer feedback gently.
- Stay on-topic and avoid unsafe or harmful ideas.
- Use inclusive, positive language.

## Not Allowed
- Harassment, hate, or discrimination.
- Sharing harmful content.
- Attempts to create operational, autonomous, or dangerous AI systems.
- Asking for or sharing technical algorithms that could enable unsafe behavior.

## Our Philosophy
The Arsik Continuum encourages ethical thinking, creativity, and symbolic
understanding. We reflect that in our interactions.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may moderate discussions to keep this space ethical,
safe, and age-appropriate.
